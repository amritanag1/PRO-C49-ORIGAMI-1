Interesting Facts about Chameleons
  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts about Pigeons
  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent birds
  2. Pigeons are reowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts about Camels
  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  3. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

Interesting Facts about Pandas
  1. Pandas are BIG eaters – every day they fill their tummies for up to 12 hours, shifting up to 12 kilograms of bamboo!
  2. The giant panda’s scientific name is Ailuropoda melanoleuca, which means “black and white cat-foot.
  3. Giant pandas are omnivores, which means their diet consists of both vegetation and meat. However, bamboo is by far their favourite food.
